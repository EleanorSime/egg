Ingredients

  • Two large free range eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • A small lump of butter
  • A handful of grated cheese

  1. Crack the eggs into a mixing bowl with a pinch of salt and pepper and beat all together.
  2. Heat the lump of butter in a small frying pan over a low flame.
  3. When the butter has melted add the beaten eggs making sure you cover the base of the pan with the mixture.
  4. Once the omelette begins to become firm slide a spatula underneath and if the underside is golden brown, flip the omelette over in the pan.
  5. SSprinkle the handful of grated cheese over the golden brown side and once the underside is also golden, slide the omelette from the pan onto your plate.
