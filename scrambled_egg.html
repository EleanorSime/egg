Poached Scrambled Omelette Scotch Boiled
Previous Next

Ingredients

  • 6 Free range eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • A small lump of butter


  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and add some freshly ground salt and pepper
  2. Beath the eggs well using a hand whisk.
  3. Put a medium sized pan on a low flame and adding the lump of butter into it, allow to melt.
  4. Add the beaten eggs into the pan and stir continuously until the mixture is half hard half liquid and take the pan off the flame. The heat from the pan will then finish cooking the eggs.
