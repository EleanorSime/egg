Ingredients

  • Free range eggs


  1. Bring a pan of water to a rolling boil
  2. Drop a room temperature egg into the pan of boiling water for the following times for:
    Soft boiled egg good for egg soldiers: 5 minutes
    Medium boiled egg good for a salad: 7 minutes
    Hard boiled egg good for mayo: 9 minutes
