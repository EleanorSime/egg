Poached Scrambled Omelette Scotch Boiled
Previous Next

Ingredients

  • Free range eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper


  1. Using a wide saucepan, fill it with boiling water and bring it to a gentle simmer. The bubbles should not be too big as they will rupture the egg. Add a pinch of salt to the water.
  2. Crack your egg into a bowl and then gently plop it into the water in one go. It will begin to cook as soon as it hits the water. Don't worry if it looks a bit messy, it will look fine when you scoop it out.
  3. A really soft poached egg should take 2 minutes and a soft to firm one will take 4 minutes.
  4. To remove from the pan, use a slotted spoon to carefully scoop them out of the water and allow to dry.
  5. When it's dry, serve with freshly ground salt and pepper.
